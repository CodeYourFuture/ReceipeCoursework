Authentic tasty Nepali Momo

Momo (dumplings) is one of Nepal's most popular dishes which can be eaten as an entree or as mains. It's a dumpling filled with meat or vegetables as well. It is eaten with tomato pickle (golbheda ko achar).

order
Ingredients
  • 250 gm of minced meat
  • 1 grated onion
  • Some finely chopped coriander
  • 1 tablespoons of ginger and garlic paste each
  • Half a tablespoon of coriander powder
  • Half a tablespoon of turmeric
  • Half a tablespoon of cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoons of grated chilli
  • 1 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 kg of flour
  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seed
  • 4 medium tomatoes, chopped
Method
  • Mix the fillings
  • Make a dough and wrapper
  • put the fillings into wrapper
  • chopped tomato n cooed with seasom seed
  • Blend tomato until smooth.
  • Serve the momos with the dipping chutney.
