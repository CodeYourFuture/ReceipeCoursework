Butter Chicken recipe

Country of origin

Butter chicken originated from Northern India in 1948. Created by Kundan Lal Gujral. Similar to Chicken Tikka Masala, Butter Chicken is one of the most popular dish from india and Pakistanwhich is very popular around the world. Aromatic golden chicken pieces in an incredible creamy curry sauce, this Butter Chicken recipe is one of the best you will try! You will love how easy it is to make in the comfort of your own home.
